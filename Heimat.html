• Heimat
    • In English and also in many other languages there is no identifier for the German word «Heimat», which describes the feeling of home and the local origin of a person at the same time. I wanted to highlight this contradiction with my work in the first semester. For me, «Heimat» as an abstract term remained a surreal idea and a philosophical construction. The newspaper shows photographs of roads in the background (as a metaphor for the path of searching) and a surreal place in the foreground (as a metaphor for the non-existence of this term). The images work with questions from the questionnaire of Max Frisch, a Swiss author.
