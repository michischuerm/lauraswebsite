• KFZ
    • The bookstore Mattmann in Zofingen, Switzerland, asked our collective Block 11 (Yoshiya Abiko and me) for our collaboration in a writing competition. They searched for text material about the legend of the king of Zofingen for a theatre performance at the old town. We did four A2 posters in store windows and one A3 poster that can also be used as a flyer. The posters were designed typographically.
